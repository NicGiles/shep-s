Bar Manager Ophélie Del Vecchio and owner James 'Shep' Shepherd create a new Cocktail List every month.

Most of the drinks are based on classic recipes: some are kept intact and some are tweaked using a variety of spirits, liqueurs and exciting ingredients. We believe in offering our guests a balanced menu that incorporates some traditional favourites yet also suggests modern innovation.

We also host our weekly event, 'Shep's Happy Mondays' where we serve a discounted Drink Special based on a spirit that currently excites us.
